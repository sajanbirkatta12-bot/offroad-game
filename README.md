# 🏎️ Offroad Game

An exciting top-down offroad racing game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript!

## 🎮 Features

- **Top-Down Racing** - Classic arcade-style perspective
- **Vehicle Physics** - Realistic acceleration, friction, and rotation
- **Multiple Obstacle Types** - Rocks, water, and mud with different damage values
- **Collectible Items** - Coins, stars, and health packs
- **Progressive Difficulty** - Game gets harder as you drive further
- **Health System** - Manage your vehicle's health by avoiding collisions
- **Score Tracking** - Track distance, speed, score, and level
- **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop and mobile

## 🕹️ How to Play

### Controls
- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** - Move and rotate vehicle
- **SPACE** - Brake

### Game Objects

**Collectibles (Gain Points)**
- 🪙 Coin = 50 points
- ⭐ Star = 100 points
- 🩹 Health Pack = Restore 25 HP

**Obstacles (Take Damage)**
- 🪨 Rock = 10 damage
- 💧 Water = 20 damage
- 🌪️ Mud = 15 damage

### Objective
🏁 Drive as far as possible while collecting items and avoiding obstacles!

## 📊 Stats Display

- **Distance** - How far you've traveled (m)
- **Speed** - Current vehicle velocity (km/h)
- **Score** - Total points collected
- **Level** - Current difficulty level (increases every 5000m)
- **Health** - Vehicle health percentage

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Clone or download this repository
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. Start playing!

## 🎯 Tips

- Collect health packs to keep your vehicle alive
- Higher levels mean more obstacles - stay alert!
- Stars are worth twice as much as coins
- Plan your route to avoid multiple obstacles
- The longer you survive, the higher your score!
